Board Game Website

Welcome to Board Game Website! We are a site that aims to give back to the board game community though high quality, accessible recommendations, as well as a suite of organizational tools for anyone with a physical library they want to lend out.

New and old board gamers alike will find value in our site’s recommendation algorithm. These recommendations can either be tailor made based on the games you’ve liked on our site, or games similar to a certain game can be easily accessed from that game’s detailed page. Users also create custom collections, groups of games curated as recommendations or any type of ‘playlist’ if you will.

Those with an established physical collection that they wish to lend out will also get a lot of use out of our library feature. You can register copies of each of your games, and then when you lend them out whoever you lend them out to can check that copy out from your library, so you know where your copies are at all times!

Thanks so much to the 316 staff for assisting us along the way and for the wonderful semester!
